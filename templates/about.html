About GSURF

Building resilient, inclusive and sustainable cities through research, collaboration and innovation.

Who We Are

The Global South Urban Resilience Forum (GSURF) is a multidisciplinary platform committed to strengthening urban resilience through research, knowledge sharing, innovation and strategic partnerships.

We collaborate with governments, universities, civil society, development partners and local communities to create sustainable, inclusive and climate-resilient cities across the Global South.
