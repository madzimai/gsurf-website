About GSURF

Building resilient, inclusive and sustainable cities through research, collaboration and innovation.

Who We Are

The Global South Urban Resilience Forum (GSURF) is dedicated to promoting sustainable urban development through research, policy engagement, knowledge sharing and strategic partnerships.

We collaborate with governments, universities, civil society, development partners and local communities to strengthen resilience across cities in the Global South.

Our Mission

To advance resilient urban development through research, innovation, policy support and capacity building.

Our Vision

A future where cities across the Global South are resilient, inclusive, sustainable and prosperous.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We uphold transparency and accountability.

Innovation

We embrace creative solutions to urban challenges.

Collaboration

Working together for lasting impact.

Sustainability

Creating resilient cities for future generations.
